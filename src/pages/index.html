Not your typical VTOL

This machine transitions seamlessly and continuously from hover to fast forward winged flight (and back) resulting in the most unique flying experience yet! You are flying like a quad and a plane all the time: there is no 'flying mode' switch like other VTOL's. With the new continuously variable transition system you pitch the quad to fly forward while the plane maintains your commanded angle of attack. The faster you fly, the more lift you get from the wings. You actually have to reduce throttle when you pitch the quad to maintain altitude! This is the plane you fly like a racing quad.
